Welcome to TolkienSoc!

We're the only Smial in Bristol something something more intro text. We run a lot of events and get togethers. Founded in 2014, this is our first year as a society!

We do, of course, hold all day movie marathons on a yearly basis. Join us for 12 hours of orc decapitation, pizza and good company!

Secondly, we have a library of books and board games that users are free to borrow. Take a look and make a request at our library page.

We also hold crafts evenings from time to time. Come create a staff, knit a scarf or even create yourself a lovely dwarven beard!

Possible our most popular evenings are our semi-regular board game evenings. We have several games based on Tolkien's works and we encourage members to bring their own, Tolkien related or not.

Lastly, but certainly not least, we have other social gatherings throughout the year. From pub crawls to Tolkien Reading Day, cinema trips to TV marathons, there's bound to be something you'll enjoy.


Most of our events are organised via our Facebook group, so join to find out what we're doing next. We also have a mailing list that all society members are automatically added to when they join.
