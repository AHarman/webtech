Library

Here you can browse through our collection of Tolkien's works and books related to his works.

All books here are available to be borrowed by any member of the society. If you wish to borrow a book, please contact the committe or ask on the Facebook group and we'll get it to you as soon as we can.

We are slowly building up this collection, so if you want to donate a book (or see a cheap copy somewhere around Bristol!) then contact the committee.
